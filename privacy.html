Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 May 2026

This page explains what data WeOffer Growth collects when you visit our website or contact us, and what we do with it. We try to keep this in plain English. If anything is unclear, write to weoffer.info@gmail.com.

1. Who we are

WeOffer Growth is a small engineering studio based in Pune, India. We build automation, AI systems, and custom internal tools for businesses. Reach us at weoffer.info@gmail.com or on WhatsApp at +91 98813 68336.

2. What we collect

We only collect data you give us, plus a small amount your browser sends automatically.

  • Form submissions. Name, email, phone, business name, and the message you send via the contact or audit form.
  • Technical data. Page URL, referring page, and timestamp. We do not run third-party analytics by default. If we add tools like Google Analytics or Meta Pixel later, we will update this page.
  • Project data. If you become a client, we receive the data needed to do the work, like access to your tools, sample records, or files. We treat this as confidential.

3. How we use it

  • To reply to your enquiry and schedule the call you asked for.
  • To improve how the site works and how clear our offers are.
  • To send you follow-ups about the project you are discussing with us. We do not add you to a marketing list. We do not sell your data.

4. Where it sits

Form submissions land in a private Google Sheet and are emailed to our team inbox. We use Google Workspace, WhatsApp Business, and standard developer tools. Project data lives inside your own accounts wherever possible, so you remain the owner.

5. Sharing

We do not share your information with anyone outside our team, except where it is needed to deliver the service you have asked for, or where required by law. If we ever bring in a sub-contractor on your project, we will tell you first.

6. Cookies

The site does not set cookies for tracking. Your browser may store small bits of data for performance, but nothing identifies you personally. If this changes, we will add a banner.

7. Your rights

You can ask us at any time to show you what we hold about you, correct it, or delete it. Email weoffer.info@gmail.com with the subject "Data request" and we will reply within seven working days.

8. Changes to this policy

If we update this page, the date at the top will change. Material changes will be flagged on the home page for at least two weeks.
